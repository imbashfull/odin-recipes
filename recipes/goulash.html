American Goulash

Description

This Americanized version of goulash was invented to stretch a small amount of beef into enough food for a not-so-small family. It's a simple dish that does'nt taste so simple, so it's perfect for dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 quarter chicken broth
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can diced tomato
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup chopped italian parsley
  • 1 cupped shredded white Cheddar cheese
