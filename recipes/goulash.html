American Goulash

Description

This Americanized version of goulash was invented to stretch a small amount of beef into enough food for a not-so-small family. It's a simple dish that does'nt taste so simple, so it's perfect for dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 quarter chicken broth
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can diced tomato
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup chopped italian parsley
  • 1 cupped shredded white Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heal oil in a pot over medium-heat. add ground beef and onion;cook and stir until the beef is browned and crumbly and onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. continue to cook until liquid is evaporated. add all the seasonings.
  3. stir occasionally, until flavors come together, about 3 minutes. Pour in broth, marinara sauce, diced tomatoes. Pour water into the sauce jar, swirl, and pour into the pot. Stir in soy sauce; Bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to medium and simmer until the flavor intensifies, about 30 minutes.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and bring to a rapid simmer. Stir in macaroni;cook, stirring occasionally, until just barely tender, about 12 minutes. Check for doneness after 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from the heat and discard bay leaves. Stir in cheddar and parsley. Cover and let rest for 5 minutes. Taste and season as desired.
