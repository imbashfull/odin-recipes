Pancake

Description

This pancake recipe makes thick and fluffy pancakes with just a few ingredients in your pantry.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter into a hot pan
  4. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes then flip
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides
