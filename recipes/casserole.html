Casserole

Description

Tasty casserole with layers ofsauerkraut, corned beef, swiss cheese, rye bread crumbs, and russian-style salad dressing

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of rye bread
  • 1 can sauerkraut, drained and rinsed
  • 1 pound deli sliced corned beef, cut into strips
  • 3/4 cup russian-style salad dressing
  • Cooking spray
  • 2 cups shredded Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C
  2. Spread bread in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Layer the ingredients. Cover with aluminum foil
  3. Baked in preheated oven for over 20 minutes. Remove and discard foil. Sprinkle Swiss cheese over casserole. Continue baking until cheese is melted and bubbly.
